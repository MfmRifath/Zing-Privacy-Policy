Zing Privacy Policy

At Zing, your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this policy carefully to understand how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information: When shop owners create an account and subscribe to our services, we collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business/store information
  • Payment information (handled by PayHere)

b. Non-Personal Information: We may collect non-personal information such as:

  • Device information (e.g., device type, operating system)
  • App usage data (e.g., pages visited, features used)
  • Location data (for store marketing purposes)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Zing app and services.
  • Process payments through the PayHere payment gateway.
  • Improve the app’s performance and user experience.
  • Communicate with shop owners regarding updates, promotions, and support.
  • Facilitate store marketing by displaying shop details to customers.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers like PayHere for payment processing.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Payment Information

Payments in the Zing app are processed securely through the PayHere payment gateway. Zing does not store any payment information (e.g., credit card details). PayHere may collect and store payment information as per their privacy and security policies. Please review PayHere’s privacy policy for details on their data handling practices.

5. Security of Your Information

We prioritize the security of your personal information. We use industry-standard security measures, including encryption, to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you wish to deactivate your account, we will retain your information only as necessary to comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal or contractual limitations).

To exercise these rights, please contact us at shahil@zingmarketingmastery.com.

8. Third-Party Links

The Zing app may contain links to third-party websites. This Privacy Policy does not apply to those third-party websites, and we are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app, and your continued use of the Zing app following any updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at shahil@zingmarketingmastery.com.
